# F1® Smart Bike Light

A universal bike break light designed to improve safety during day and night time operation.

## Description

A F1® rain light inspired bike break light
#### Features
  * Adaptive breaking signals your rate of breaking to surrounding drivers and pedestrians 
  * Automatic Day/Night mode
  * Removable for longer battery life and safety
  * Makes your bike look cooler
  

### Instalation

Mount the bike specific mount to your bikes seat post or bike rack.
The light then securely locks into to the bracket and is easily removable for parking and charging.

* The light automatically goes ito its deepest sleep mode when removed from the bike in this setting this goes it around ~140 (subject to testing) days of operation on a full charge.

* The light automatically adjusts to to local lighting contitions to switch into night mode ( make sure not to cover the sensor for this located at the top to the right of the charging port. 

### Battery life 

* All estimates are subject to testing 

~140 days deep sleep mode

~ 5 hours daytime running

~ 3 hours night time use


## Help

If you are unable to connect the device to your computer to push updates; try holding a magnet near the back to wake up the device from sleep.
